About Me

I can still remember the words uttered from under my dad's bristly mustache, "When are you going to get off that damn computer and get a real job?" I was never one for following the typical archetype for life. I was curious. When I got a new birthday present the first thing I would do was try to modify it, make it cooler or take it apart to see how it worked.

My name is Eliud Arbelo II. I am a self-taught, multidisciplinary designer with over 10 years of experience in identity, branding, print collateral, packaging, web design, UX/UI and other digital mediums. My experience also extends to management of remote teams and PMO of product development. I'm still on the computer, still making things cooler, still taking things apart to see how they work.
